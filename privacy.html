Privacy Policy

Teleprompter Studio · Last updated: July 5, 2026

Your privacy matters. In short: Teleprompter Studio does not collect your personal data.

Data we collect

Teleprompter Studio does not collect, store on our servers, or share any personal information. No accounts, no login, no analytics or advertising trackers.

Data stored on your device

Your scripts, settings and preferences are stored locally on your device only. They never leave your phone and are not accessible to us. Deleting the app removes this data.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to verify subscription status; for this, an anonymous identifier and your purchase/receipt information are processed to confirm an active subscription. We do not receive your name, email, or payment details. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy and Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children

Teleprompter Studio is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.
