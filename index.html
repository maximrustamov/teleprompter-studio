Frequently asked

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are handled by Apple: iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Teleprompter Studio. You can also tap "Restore Purchases" in the app's Settings.

How do I import a script?
Copy your text anywhere, open a new script in the editor, and tap the paste button in the top-right.

Will I lose my scripts?
Your scripts are stored on your device. Keep the app installed to keep them.
